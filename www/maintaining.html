Maintaining a Happy, Healthy, and Productive Workforce

General Tips

  • Take time to get to know your employees.
  • Listen to your employees.
  • Look for positive behaviours in employees and email or talk to them ASAP.
  • End team meetings on a positive note.
  • Provide a team building event at least once a year (e.g., potluck dinner; bowling)
  • Maintain an open-door policy.
  • Order healthy snacks and water for long or boring meetings.
  • Celebrate years or service or other milestones with your team.
  • Send "thank you for all you do" notes when things are especially hectic.
  • Be Creative! Have fun!
