Disability Leave

If and when the employee is on disability leave:

  • DO communicate with the employee about every two weeks
  • DO communicate openly with the employee-limits misunderstandings and prevents barriers
  • DO invite the employee to social events (e.g., pot luck dinner or Holiday party)
  • DO coordinate with Human Resources about who is doing what and when
  • DO offer the same support to employees with mental health problems as you do to employees with physical health problems
  • DO offer as much reassurance as possible (e.g., about the employee's job security)
  • DO NOT rush their return back to the workplace
  • DO NOT talk about too many work-related issues or events
