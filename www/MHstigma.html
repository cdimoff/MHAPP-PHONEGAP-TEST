Mental Health Stigma

  • Most Canadians feel more comfortable with an individual who has a physical health problem than an individual who has a mental health problem.
  • 2 in 3 people with a mental health problem do not seek or receive treatment due to the fear of being discriminated against, judged, and/or rejected.
  • 46% of Canadians think people use mental illness as an excuse for bad behaviour.
  • 70%: The misdiagnosis rate of mental health problems due to stigma.
  • Fear of discrimination: The most significant factor preventing employees from seeking mental health support, treatment, or accommodation from their workplace.
