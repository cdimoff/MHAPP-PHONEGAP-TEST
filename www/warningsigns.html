Warning Signs of a Mental Health Problem

Behavioural Warning Signs

  • Missing Deadlines
  • Erratic Behaviour
  • Emotional Responses
  • Complaints about Workload
  • Withdrawing from Colleagues
  • Reduced Participation in Work
  • Unplanned or Frequent Absences
  • Increased Errors or Accidents
  • Decreased Productivity
  • Excessive Overtime

Psychological Warning Signs

  • Inability to Concentrate
  • Emotional Instability
  • Indecisiveness
  • Lack of Confidence
  • Less Interest in Work
  • Strange or Grandiose Ideas

Physical Warning Signs

  • Extreme Fatigue
  • Headaches
  • Emotional outburts
  • Difficulty Sleeping
  • Weight Loss or Gain
  • Dishevelled Appearance
  • Frequent Physical Illness
