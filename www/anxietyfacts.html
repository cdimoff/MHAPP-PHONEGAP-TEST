Anxiety Facts

  • Anxiety disorders are the most common mental health problem in Canada.
  • 9% of men and 16% of women are diagnosed with an anxiety problem each year

Types of Anxiety

  • General Anxiety Disorder.
  • Phobic Disorders.
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorders.
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder.
  • Panic Attacks.

Anxiety Causes and Treatments

  • Social
  • Pyschological
  • Biological
