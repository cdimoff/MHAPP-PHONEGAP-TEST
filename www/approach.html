How to Approach Employees: A Tip Sheet

Tips on How to Approach

  • DO be respectful of your employee's privacy
  • DO emphasize abilities, not limitations
  • DO remain calm and focused on performance issues, not the individual's personality
  • DO address specific concerns related to employee's performance, workplace actions, or behaviour
  • DO offer support and resources
  • DO stay calm, but don't "carry on"

Tips on How NOT to Approach

  • DO NOT offer a pep talk
  • DO NOT be accusatory or blame the employee
  • DO NOT label or diagnose the issue
  • DO NOT use labels or terms such as "retarded", "crazy", or "psychotic"
  • DO NOT condone, promote, or allow stigmatization or discrimination
  • DO NOT probe for personal information or details
