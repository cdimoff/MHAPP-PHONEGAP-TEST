What to do regarding disability/accommodations

  • DO tell people how it affects them
  • DO listen to to your employees and their concerns
  • Do communicate with other employees
    • Workload distributions
    • Team projects
  • Do address team member feelings (e.g., anger or resentment; empathy/sympathy)
    • Anger and resentment can be met with "Disapproving parent response"
  • DO establish a "sunshine fund" ahead of time
    • Flowers, cards, visits, etc.
  • DO find out what the employee out on leave would like their colleagues to be told
    • Example: "Jon is going to be out of the office for the next little while for health-related reasons" or "Mary is now going to be working on the computer instead of the phones for the next little while for health-related reasons"
  • DO be aware that Facebook and Twitter can be sources of problems
    • Use cancer comparison and "disapproving parent" approach
