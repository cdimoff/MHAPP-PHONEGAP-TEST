Tips for Preventing Stress

General Tips

  • Recognize warning signs of excessive stress at work (See "Stress Warning Signs")
  • Give employees the opportunity to participate in decisions that impact them
  • Establish and enforce a zero-tolerance policy for harassment and bullying
  • Provide opportunities for social interaction among employees
  • Break projects into smaller steps to make them seem less daunting and more manageable
  • Look for humor in stressful situations at work

Be aware of the common causes of workplace stress (Expanding)

  • Fear of being laid off
  • Downsizing and overtime
  • Pressure to perform at optimal levels at all times

Improve communication between yourself and your employees (Expanding)

  • Share information with employees to reduce uncertainty
  • Define employees' roles and responbilities
  • Make communication friendly and efficient, not mean-spirited or argumentative

Take care of yourself (Expanding)

  • Exercise-Try to get 30 minutes of activity each day
  • Eat Healthy-Eat small, but frequent meals throughout the day
  • Sleep-Try to get 7-8 hours of restful sleep each night
  • Drink alcohol in moderation and avoid nicotine

Prioritize and organizes your responsibilities-both at home and at work (Expanding)

  • Create a balanced schedule-All work and no play is a recipe for burnout
  • Don't over-commit yourself-Avoid scheduling things back to back
  • Plan regular breaks-5 to 15 minutes
