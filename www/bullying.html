Others: Stigma and Bullying

Tips for dealing with others regarding stigma/bullying:

  • Be a leader and educate your team-Raise awareness about mental health and mental health problems
  • Focus on communication-Improved communication can help reduce fear, stigma, and discrimination
  • Treat everyone equally-whether they have a physical, mental, or unknown health problem
  • Speak up-Respectfully address derogatory, mean-spirited, hurtful, or stigmatizing comments about mental illness or people with mental health problems
