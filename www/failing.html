Failing to Approach Employees with Warning Signs: The Consequences

The consequences can include...

  • A strained relationship between you and the employee
  • Angry or resentful coworkers who shun the employee
  • An employee with a mental health condition does not get the support that they need
  • An unnecessary deterioration in an employee's health and performance at work
  • Deterioration in morale, which is likely to affect all employees
  • Safety risks for the individual and other employees
  • Increased absenteeism and greater disability costs
  • The inappropriate and potentially unlawful termination of the employee
  • The avoidable loss of a skilled worker
