Prevent Mental Health Problems

Tips for Preventing Mental Health Problems:

  • Ask employees how they are doing at least 2 twice a week.
  • Provide employees with as much job control as possible.
  • Give your employees "pat on the back" rewards.
  • Be a good role model when it comes to mental health.
  • Don't use inappropriate or stigmatizing language.
  • Discourage gossip, rumours, and stigmatizing behaviours
  • Communicate with your employees
  • Raise awareness and encorage discussion about mental health and wellbeing.
  • Encourage appropriate breaks from work.
  • Allow flexibility in working arrangements.
  • Praise staff and provide positive feedback for good work.
  • Celebrate shared accomplishments.
