Tips for Preventing Burnout

General Tips

  • Recognize the warning signs of burnout
  • Set boundaries-learn to say "no" to requests
  • Take a daily break from technology
    • Set a time each day when you completely disconnect
  • Re-evalute your goals and priorities
    • Are you focusing your time and energy on things that are most important you and your goals, dreams, and hopes?
  • Take a proactive approach when addressing workplace issues
    • It will make you feel less helpless
