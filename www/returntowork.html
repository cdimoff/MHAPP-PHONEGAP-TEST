Return to Work Plan

When an employee is returning to work after being on disability leave:

  • DO communicate with the employee while he or she is away
  • DO communicate with the employee about accommodations upon return
  • DO ask the employee which accommodations might work best
  • DO communicate with HR to develop the most appropriate accommodation plan
    • Remember, you and your employee know the work and the workplace best
  • DO schedule a conversation with the employee before he/she returns to the workplace
    • Update him or her on work developments and social life
  • DO follow-up with the employee after he/she has returned to the workplace
    • A 2-week, 4 week, and 6 week follow-up is best
  • DO make expectations and work roles clear when the employee returns to work
  • DO re-evaluate the return-to-work plan to make sure it's working
  • DO be patient with your employee and everyone else involved
  • DO be a role model-welcome the employee and take action against stigma
  • DO set and re-visit goals with the employees
