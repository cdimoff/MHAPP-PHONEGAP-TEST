General Mental Health Information

Mental Health Problems....

  • are caused by a combination of genetic, biological, and environmental factors.
  • affect people of all ages, education, and economic backgrounds.
  • 65% of managers say they could do their job more effectively if they had employee mental health training.
  • are the second leading cause of disability and premature death in Canada.
  • cost the Canadian economy $52 billion every year.

Did you know that...?

  • 1 in 5 Canadians suffers from a mental health problem every year
  • Most people who are treated for mental health problems will fully recover
