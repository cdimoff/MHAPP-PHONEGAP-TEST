Others: Return to Work

  • DO prevent and stop any discrimination or stigmatization from coworkers
  • DO prepare the employee who has been on disability leave for questions from coworkers
  • DO prepare employees for the return of the employee who has been away
    • Tell them how the employee's return affects them
  • DO suggest that the employee returning to work prepare a response to these questions
    • Example: "Yes, I was away for health-related reasons, and it was difficult, but I'm so glad to be back now. I'll be working on a restricted work week, but soon I'll be back 100%--I'm really looking forward to it. How have you been?"
