Anxiety Warning Signs

Observable Signs:

  • Vomiting
  • Poor Concentration
  • Irritability
  • Impatience

Less Observable Signs:

  • Chest Pain
  • Rapid Heartbeat
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Dizziness
  • Sweating
  • Numbness and Tingling
  • Nausea
  • Excessive Fear
  • Racing Thoughts
  • Sleep Disturbances
