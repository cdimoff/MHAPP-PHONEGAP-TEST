Stress Facts

  • Stress: "A state of mental or emotional strain or tension resulting from adverse or demanding circumstances."
  • All types of stress carry physical and mental health risks.
  • Changes in health due to routine stress are often hardest to recognize.
  • Different people respond to stress differently.

3 Types of Stress

  • Routine Stress-related to pressures of work, family, and other daily responsibilities.
  • Sudden stress-brought on by sudden negative change, such as divorce or illness.
  • Traumatic stress-triggered by an event, such as war or natural disaster
