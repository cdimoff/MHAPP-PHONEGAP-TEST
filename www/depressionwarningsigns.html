Depression Warning Signs

Observable Signs:

  • Reduced Work Quality
  • Reduced Work Quantity
  • Deterioration in Appearance
  • Weight Gain
  • Weight Loss
  • Tearfulness
  • Fatigue

Less Observable Signs:

  • Loss of Interest
  • Loss of Motivation
  • Difficulty Concentrating
  • Insomnia
  • Feelings of Worthlessness
  • Low Self-Esteem
  • Suicidal Thoughts
  • Persistant Sadness
