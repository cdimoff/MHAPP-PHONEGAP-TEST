Burnout Facts

  • Burnout: "Long-term exhaustion and diminished interest in work".

Phases of Burnout

  1. Compulsion to prove oneself-excessive ambition
  2. High personal expectations and work ethic
  3. Neglect of personal needs (e.g., family, health)
  4. Social isolation and problem avoidance
  5. Denial of emerging problem.
  6. Withdrawal from work and personal life
